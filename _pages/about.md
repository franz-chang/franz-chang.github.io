<span class="anchor" id="about-me"></span>

**Chong Zhang** is currently pursuing a PhD at the **Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University** & **University of Liverpool**, with research interests in **Agent Simulation, LLM Safety, Explainability for LLM, and Recommender systems**. He has delivered presentations at prestigious conferences, including EMNLP, ICDM, ECAI, ICME and PRCV, and actively contributes to the academic community as a reviewer for renowned journals and conferences.
